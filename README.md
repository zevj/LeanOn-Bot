📌 **LeanOn Bot**
An AI-Assisted Mental Health Wellness Support System for Students

📖 **Project Overview**

LeanOn Bot is a web-based, AI-assisted mental health support system designed specifically for students of Gordon College. 
It provides a safe, confidential, and judgment-free platform where students can express their concerns and receive immediate, wellness-focused responses.

The system aims to improve accessibility to mental health support by offering 24/7 digital assistance, 
helping students manage stress, anxiety, and emotional challenges—while complementing the services of the Guidance and Counseling Unit.

This project aligns with:

SDG 3 – Good Health and Well-being

SDG 4 – Quality Education

🎯 **Objectives**

General Objective

To design and develop a centralized AI-assisted mental wellness platform that provides accessible, confidential, and real-time emotional support for students.

Specific Objectives

Provide real-time chatbot responses for emotional concerns
Detect stress, anxiety, and distress patterns through text input
Recommend coping strategies (e.g., breathing, grounding, relaxation)
Offer automated referral suggestions to the Guidance Office
Deliver anonymized reports for administrative monitoring

🚨 **Problem Statement**

The Guidance and Counseling Unit currently faces challenges such as:

Difficulty identifying students who need help
Inefficient handling of student concerns
Limited monitoring of counseling activities
Lack of a centralized and automated system

Additionally, many students:

Avoid face-to-face counseling due to stigma or fear of judgment
Need support beyond office hours

💡 **Proposed Solution**

LeanOn Bot addresses these issues by introducing:

🤖 AI-powered chatbot for emotional support

🔍 Emotion recognition & distress detection

🧘 Wellness recommendation engine

📩 Referral system to Guidance Office

🔐 Secure and confidential user environment

📊 Admin dashboard with anonymized insights

⚙️ **Key Features**
1. AI Chatbot Interaction
Real-time responses to student concerns
Safe, private, and judgment-free conversation
2. Emotion Recognition System
Detects stress, anxiety, and emotional patterns
Identifies repeated distress signals
3. Wellness Recommendation Engine
Breathing exercises
Grounding techniques
Stress management tips
Positive affirmations
4. Referral Recommendation System
Suggests professional help when needed
Encourages consultation with Guidance Office
5. Admin Dashboard
Displays anonymized emotional trends
Tracks system usage statistics
6. Web-Based Access
Mobile-responsive design
Accessible anytime with internet

🧠 **Scope and Limitations**

✅ Scope

AI-assisted emotional support
Text-based chatbot interaction
Student-focused mental wellness guidance
Secure and confidential system

❌ Limitations

Does NOT replace licensed mental health professionals
Does NOT provide medical diagnosis
No real-time human counselor chat
No emergency crisis intervention (initial version)
Requires internet connection
Text-based only (no voice interaction)

🧩 **System Architecture (Overview)**

The system follows an Input–Process–Output (IPO) Model:

Input: User messages, emotional concerns

Process: Emotion detection, response generation

Output: Chatbot replies, coping strategies, referrals
